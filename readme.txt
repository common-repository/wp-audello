== Description ==

Easily embed Audello files, playlists, audio widgets and audio bars into WordPress posts, even with the visual editor! All you need to do is enter your Audello details into the plugin's settings, and you'll be able to point and click to embed right into your posts without messing with any code.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `wp-audello` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Click on the Settings link, either underneath the plugin in the list, or inside the Settings area
4. Enter the subdomain for your Audello account (for example, 'yourname')
5. Enter your password
6. Press the 'Save' button - if there are no errors, you're ready to proceed to make a new post or page
7. Click the Audello logo when editing or creating a post or page to bring up your list of files
8. Click on the name of the file to embed it into your post

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I embed audio files into my posts? =

Set up the plugin as explained in the installation instructions, and then click on the button on the post (or page!) editor. The plugin will fetch your list of files right from your Audello installation and you can click on them to insert them into your post.
